About Python Kitchen

Our story & values

Who we are

With over 25 years experience of supplying and installing kitchens and bedrooms in and all around the London area, Python Kitchen has established itself as an accomplished kitchens and bedrooms designer. We also provide the services to replace simple doors, worktops and kitchen facelifts to brand new kitchen installations.

We offer our customers a wide selection of range of kitchen styles to chose from. Our specialization not only encompasses modern, high gloss, handle less and shaker style kitchens but also contemporary, classic and traditional kitchens.

All of our kitchens are manufactured in the UK and therefore, we can usually have our kitchens supplied and fitted within 4 to 8 weeks of placing an order. The supply-only option is increasingly popular with a lot of our clients and we will work with your builders or fitters to ensure that our kitchens are fitted correctly as per the plans provided.
